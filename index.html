Words You'll Hear

The handful of terms worth knowing on day one.

License
Your state insurance license — the credential that lets you legally help families with life insurance. You earn it by passing a state exam (we get you there in about two weeks).
Contracting
Getting appointed with the insurance carriers so you can actually write policies. We handle this in the background while you study.
Commission / 1099
You're an independent business owner, not an employee — you earn commission on the business you write, set your own hours, and build something that's yours.
Hell Week
Your launch week — one focused week of long days on the phones with a mentor on your calls, aimed at landing your first sale fast.
Persistency
How well your policies stay active over time. Writing solid business families keep is what builds a healthy, lasting income.
Mentorship
You're never on your own — someone who's done this is on your first calls with you until you're confident.
